Geothermal Basics

Understanding the science of heat and movement.

What is thermal energy?

Thermal energy is energy associated with heat. As you heat up a substance, its atoms move faster and its thermal energy increases.

How do we move it?

A heat pump is a device that moves thermal energy from one place to another. It provides both heating and cooling.

Air-Source: Moves energy between inside and outside air.

Ground-Source: Uses stable underground temperatures year-round.
